MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT (MVP)

The MVP version of ShieldOps will include:

CORE FEATURES:

1. Admin Login
2. Client Management (Add/View)
3. Staff Management (Add/View)
4. Contract Creation
5. Staff Deployment
6. Attendance Entry
7. Invoice Generation
8. Payment Recording

EXCLUDED FROM MVP:
• Dashboard analytics
• Advanced UI
• Notifications
• Reports

GOAL:
To demonstrate complete workflow:

Client → Contract → Deployment → Attendance → Invoice → Payment

/project
  /backend
  /frontend
  /database
  app.py
